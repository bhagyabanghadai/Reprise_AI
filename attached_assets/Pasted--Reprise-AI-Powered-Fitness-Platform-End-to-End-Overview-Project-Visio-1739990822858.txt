### **🔥 Reprise – AI-Powered Fitness Platform (End-to-End Overview) 🔥**  
#### **🚀 Project Vision**  
Reprise is an **AI-driven fitness platform** designed to revolutionize the way powerlifters, bodybuilders, and general fitness enthusiasts train. It integrates **progressive overload tracking, real-time AI coaching, form analysis, and recovery recommendations** into a seamless, interactive experience.

---

## **1️⃣ Core Features Built So Far**
### **🛠 AI-Driven Workout Planning & Coaching**
✅ **AI-Powered Progressive Overload System** – Tracks past workouts and suggests weight/repetition increases for optimized strength training.  
✅ **Custom Workout Suggestions** – AI tailors workouts based on user history, body stats, and fitness level.  
✅ **Real-Time Form Analysis** – Users can upload workout videos, and AI provides feedback on posture, form, and technique.  
✅ **Recovery & Fatigue Analysis** – AI tracks workout intensity and recommends optimal rest periods and recovery strategies.

### **📊 Data Tracking & User Engagement**
✅ **Workout Logs & Performance Tracking** – Users can log their sets, reps, and weights, with AI detecting strength trends.  
✅ **Personalized Strength Goals** – Users set strength-based goals, and AI provides a roadmap to achieve them.  
✅ **3D Interactive Progress Dashboard** – A dynamic interface visualizing **strength progress, fatigue levels, and training consistency.**  
✅ **Leaderboards & Challenges** – Community-driven fitness challenges to keep users motivated.

### **🛒 Nutrition & Recovery Guidance**
✅ **AI-Powered Meal Planning** – Generates **vegetarian-friendly** meal plans tailored to **body recomposition goals.**  
✅ **Macro & Calorie Tracking** – Helps users balance their nutrition based on their training volume.  
✅ **Supplement Recommendations** – AI suggests supplements based on **training intensity and dietary preferences.**  
✅ **Wearable Integration (Planned)** – Syncs with **smartwatches and fitness trackers** to enhance real-time insights.

## **2️⃣ Technical Stack**
### **🔗 Frontend (Web & Mobile)**
- **React.js & Tailwind CSS** (For sleek, responsive UI)  
- **React Native (Planned)** (For mobile app)  
- **3D & AR Features (Future Expansion)**  

### **💡 Backend & AI**
- **FastAPI / Flask** (For API services)  
- **Python (Core AI Models)**  
- **TensorFlow / PyTorch** (For form analysis & recommendations)  
- **AWS Lambda & S3** (For cloud-based AI processing & storage)  
- **PostgreSQL / Firebase** (For user data and workout logs)  

### **🔐 Security & Performance**
- **OAuth 2.0 & JWT Authentication** (For secure logins)  
- **End-to-End Data Encryption** (For user safety & privacy)  
- **Scalable Microservices Architecture**
